GroundIQ is Fugro's geospatial intelligence platform, synthesizing decades of subsurface survey data, satellite-derived ground motion measurements, and predictive risk models into a single interface for infrastructure planners, asset owners, and engineers.

As Lead Product Designer, I own the end-to-end product design across the platform. The work spans complex data visualization, map-based interactions, and AI-assisted analysis workflows that help clients make ground-risk decisions without needing a geotechnical PhD.
