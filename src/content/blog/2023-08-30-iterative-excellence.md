We iterate on designs. We iterate on prototypes. But we rarely iterate on the design process itself. The workflow you inherited on day one is probably not the workflow your team needs today.

Continuous improvement applied to UX process means regularly examining how you work — where the friction is, what's producing value, what's just ritual. This article covers how to audit your design process, identify improvement targets, and build a culture where process refinement is part of the job, not a distraction from it.
